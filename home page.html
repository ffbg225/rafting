ABOUT OUR SERVICE

Nestled in the heart of Sri Lanka's lush rainforest, Kithulgala offers one of the most exhilarating white water rafting experiences in South Asia.

The Kelani River provides perfect conditions for both beginners and experienced rafters, with Class II and III rapids that promise an unforgettable adventure. Our professional guides ensure your safety while you navigate through breathtaking scenery of dense tropical forests and crystal-clear waters.

After your rafting experience, explore nearby attractions including jungle waterfalls, prehistoric caves, and scenic hiking trails that showcase Sri Lanka's incredible biodiversity.
